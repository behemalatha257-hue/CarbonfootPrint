Understanding Carbon Metrics

What is CO₂e (CO₂ Equivalent)?

Greenhouse gases include carbon dioxide, methane, nitrous oxide, and others. To make tracking easy, scientists convert the warming impact of all gases into the equivalent amount of Carbon Dioxide (CO₂e).

Why is my diet footprint so large?

Livestock farming, especially beef, requires vast land, water, and feed. Cattle also emit methane, a greenhouse gas 28 times more potent than carbon dioxide over 100 years. Shifting to plant-based meals cuts diet emissions by over 50%.

How do carbon streaks and levels work?

Streaks reward consistency. Completing a committed habit daily keeps the streak active. Toggling completions rewards you with XP. Earn 100 XP to level up! Higher levels grant prestigious titles (e.g. Earth Guardian).
